Vukkybox Terms of Service

Welcome to Vukkybox! Unless otherwise specified, the terminology used in this document is the same as the Privacy Policy.
"Account" refers to the user account that you create with us when you log in, which contains information indicated in the Privacy Policy.
"Vukkies" refers to the collectable items you can get, while "boxes" refers to the items you need to purchase with Vukkybux to get Vukkies, explained below.
You need an Account to use the service.

Usage of the Service

Vukkybux

Your account is associated with your Vukkybux. This number is what you can exchange for opening different boxes to unlock Vukkies.
"Performing an action that requires being logged in" includes but is not limited to opening a box, or navigating to your profile page.
"Promo codes" refers to codes made by LIT Devs that you can redeem through the website to get free Vukkybux.
This Vukkybux can be acquired in the following ways:
  • Performing an action that requires being logged in once an hour, for 150 Vukkybux;
  • Performing an action that requires being logged in once a day, for 750 Vukkybux;
  • Redeeming promo codes;
  • Trading it for other items through our balance page.

Automation

You may not automate usage of the Vukkybox service, which includes but is not limited to automatically purchasing boxes.
Don't try to find loopholes in this policy. This policy very obviously includes (but is not limited to) sending requests to the website, and autoclickers.

Account sharing

You may not give your account to anyone to sell it, trade it, or for any other purpose.
Your account is yours, and yours only.

Responsible Bug Reporting

If you find any exploits, bugs, or other issues, please report them to us - refer to the "Contact information" section.
If the issue you find is critical, do not abuse it, or we will terminate your account.
Sharing of any critical issues outside of LIT Devs will also result in account termination.

If the issue is not critical, you may report it to an administrator in the chat, if available.

Terminations

We may terminate your Account at any time, for any reason, with or without notice.
You may terminate your Account yourself by navigating to the delete account page, as outlined in the Privacy Policy.
